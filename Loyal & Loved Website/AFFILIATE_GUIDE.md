## Notes

- **FCA Compliance**: Every insurance article needs: "We don't provide personalised financial advice. Always read the policy wording before purchasing pet insurance. [Site name] is not regulated by the FCA."
- **Affiliate Disclosure**: Already in footer of every page. Also add inline disclosure near comparison tables: "This article contains affiliate links. We may earn a commission if you sign up through our links, at no extra cost to you."
- **Horse Insurance Note**: Horse insurance is FCA-regulated just like pet insurance. Same disclaimers apply.
- **Amazon Associates**: Their terms require disclosure AND they can terminate if you don't make 3 qualifying sales within 180 days. Don't apply until you have traffic.
