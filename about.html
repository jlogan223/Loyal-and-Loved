About Loyal & Loved UK

We exist to help UK pet owners make informed, honest decisions about their pet's care and wellbeing.

Our Mission

Pet ownership in the UK comes with real decisions and real costs. Yet most pet advice online is either too generic, hidden behind paywalls, or obviously written just to promote products.

Loyal & Loved UK was created to fill that gap. We provide:

  • Honest comparisons — We compare pet insurance, food, tech, and vet care without corporate bias
  • Real UK costs — Based on actual quotes and vet prices, not generic figures
  • Expert perspective — Written by pet owners who genuinely care about animal welfare
  • No fluff — Practical, actionable advice without the marketing spin

Who We Are

Loyal & Loved UK is operated by PB Eng Ltd, a UK-registered company focused on creating useful, honest content for pet owners. We're independent and not owned by any pet insurance company or retailer.

We're pet owners ourselves — we understand the tough choices: Is pet insurance worth it? How much will a vet bill really cost? Is fresh dog food actually better? These are real questions with real financial implications, and we research them thoroughly so you don't have to.

How We Make Money

Loyal & Loved UK uses affiliate links. When you click through to pet insurance companies, retailers, or services we recommend and make a purchase, we earn a small commission. This is clearly marked throughout the site.

This does not affect your cost. You pay the same price whether you go direct or through our link.

Affiliate income allows us to spend time researching and writing in-depth guides instead of chasing advertising dollars. However, we only recommend products and services we genuinely believe are good value for UK pet owners.

What We Don't Do

  • We don't provide personalised financial or veterinary advice
  • We don't accept paid sponsorship or promotions (affiliate links only)
  • We don't recommend products purely for commission
  • We don't guarantee current prices (markets change — always verify before purchasing)
  • We don't replace professional vet advice (when in doubt, ask your vet)

Our Editorial Standards

Every guide on Loyal & Loved UK is:

  • Well-researched — Based on real UK prices, regulations, and veterinary practice
  • Transparent — All affiliates disclosed, assumptions clearly stated
  • Regularly updated — Insurance premiums, food prices, and vet costs change — we keep up
  • Honest about limitations — We acknowledge when information is incomplete or when pros need to be consulted
  • UK-focused — We use UK spelling, reference UK regulations (Pet Travel Scheme, FCA, etc.), and quote real UK prices

UK Regulatory Compliance

Loyal & Loved UK complies with:

  • GDPR: Your privacy is protected. See our Privacy Policy
  • ICMEC Code of Practice: Clear affiliate disclosures throughout
  • FCA Consumer Warnings: We include disclaimers on all insurance-related content
  • PECR: Email marketing is opt-in with easy unsubscribe

Feedback & Corrections

We aim to be accurate. If you spot an error, outdated information, or disagree with our recommendations, please get in touch. We're happy to review and correct genuine mistakes.

The Bottom Line

Loyal & Loved UK exists because we believe UK pet owners deserve honest, expert advice without corporate spin. We're independent, transparent about how we make money, and committed to providing guides that actually help you care for your pets better.
